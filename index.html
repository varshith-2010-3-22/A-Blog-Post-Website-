Psychology is the scientific study of mind and behavior . It examines mental processes (thoughts, feelings, motives) and actions in humans and animals, both conscious and unconscious . This broad discipline spans from brain function to cultural influences, seeking to explain how people think, feel, and act. By uncovering patterns in behavior and thought, psychology helps us address issues in mental health, education, and society.
